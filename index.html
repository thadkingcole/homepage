Origin

It all started in the summer of 2013. My girlfriend and I had just graduated from college, and we were looking for a way to celebrate this accomplishment and go on a fun trip together. While we could not make it to all 30 ballparks in one summer, we still wanted to go to as many as we could before needing to return to our other responsibilities. So we hopped in a car and took off for our first game.

It is probably clear based on the above that I like baseball. If you are not a fan of baseball, I will not attempt to convince you to go on a similar trip. However, going to a game is a much different experience than watching a game on TV or listening on the radio. The smell of the grass, the sounds of the game, and the movement from stillness are all reasons I love to be at games in person. At the same time, the pace of the game allows for meaningful moments to be shared between friends. If you found this site and happen to not be a baseball fan, I encourage you to go to a game with friends. There may be a minor league club much closer to you than you realize!

That girlfriend that came with me on the journey is now my wife, and you can check on our progress to all the stadiums at the various links above. I hope you enjoy your visit!
